    • Our Story

    Mary Mwaniki Foundation is a registered organization based in Kenya, dedicated to transforming the lives of people with disabilities. Our foundation was born from the personal journey of our founder, who became a person with a disability in 2008. A tragic accident, a falling tree on her way home from work, left her with severe spinal injuries. Overnight, her life transformed, and she experienced the harsh reality of disability stigma. She faced unimaginable challenges - losing her business, her home...
