Let Parents Work

(A Daycare Solution for Parents of Cerebral Palsy Kids)

This initiative aims to provide a much-needed solution for families affected by cerebral palsy, ensuring that parents can at least go earn a living while their children receive care. Our program revolves around the establishment of a specialized day care facility designed specifically for children with cerebral palsy. We understand the challenges faced by parents who strive to balance their caregiving responsibilities with their work commitments. By offering a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment, we aim to bridge this gap.
