Our Story

Mary Mwaniki Foundation is a registered organization based in Kenya, dedicated to transforming the lives of people with disabilities. Our foundation was born from the personal journey of our founder, who became a person with a disability in 2008. A tragic accident, a falling tree on her way home from work, left her with severe spinal injuries. Overnight, her life transformed, and she experienced the harsh reality of disability stigma. She faced unimaginable challenges - losing her business, her home, her friends, and her networks. The world she knew seemed to crumble around her, and she became a victim of society's negative attitudes towards people with disabilities. It was like living in a never-ending horror movie.

But she didn't let her circumstances define her. With unwavering determination, she redirected her life's purpose towards promoting the dignity and respect of persons with disabilities. She vowed to change the narrative and create a society where every individual, regardless of ability, is valued and included. Now, our foundation stands as a beacon of hope for people with disabilities, advocating for their rights and providing essential support. We invite you to join us on this journey, as your support will make a meaningful difference in the lives of those who have faced immense challenges and deserve to be treated with dignity and respect. The foundation seeks your support in its endeavors to create a world where every person can thrive, regardless of their abilities.
